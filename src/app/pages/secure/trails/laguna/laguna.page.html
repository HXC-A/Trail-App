Laguna Coast Wilderness park comprises over 7,000 acres within some of the last remaining coastal canyons of Southern California.
Sagebrush and elevation gains define much of the landscape, as well as a vast network of tamped-down hiking trails.
And any one of these routes through the wilderness park transports hikers far from the hustle and bustle of the region.
